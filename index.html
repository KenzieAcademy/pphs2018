Kenzie Academy
June 2018 PPHS Summer Coding Camp

An eager-to-learn group of fourteen rising 9th and 10th graders at Purdue Polytechnic High School Indianapolis completed a 3 week coding camp at Kenzie Academy. During this intensive course they learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and applied them to create several interactive games on their personal websites.
